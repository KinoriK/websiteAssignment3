How We Started

Our Story

Face Framers started in November 2016 with a mission to provide quality hair care to our valued customers. Over the years, we have established a close bond with our clients, ensuring that their beauty needs are met with dedication and professionalism. Our founder, Marie Sam, envisioned a space where clients could feel at home while receiving top-notch services in hairstyling, cuts, and beauty treatments. Today, Face Framers continues to grow and evolve, bringing the latest trends and personalized services to the heart of Daisy Hill.

At Face Framers, our passion for hairdressing goes beyond styles and cuts—it's about the confidence and joy our clients feel after every visit. We believe that a great hairstyle can transform not just the way you look, but how you feel.

Come and be part of our story, and let us help you achieve the look you’ve always dreamed of!

Our team has expanded over the years, bringing in professionals who are passionate about their craft. Each member of Face Framers is committed to making sure that every visit is special, from the time you walk in to the moment you leave. Our modern styling techniques, combined with a deep respect for our clients' needs, have made us one of the leading beauty destinations in the region.
